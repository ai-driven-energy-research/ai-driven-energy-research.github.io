Journal Timeline

A record of where we've been and where we're going.

February 2026
Journal founded Milestone
AIDER was established as an open-access, AI-native journal for research at the intersection of artificial intelligence and energy systems. The website, submission infrastructure, and editorial policies were built from the ground up.
February 2026
Full submission pipeline tested Milestone
The end-to-end submission and review process was tested, from GitHub issue submission through AI-assisted review to publication with a Zenodo DOI. The pipeline is now operational.
March 2026
First paper published Milestone
Foundation Models for Clean Energy Systems became AIDER's first published paper, archived on Zenodo with a permanent DOI.
March 2026
ISSN application submitted Pending
Application submitted to the ISSN UK Centre (British Library). An ISSN will give the journal a formal serial identifier recognised by libraries and indexing services worldwide.
Q2 2026
Crossref membership Planned
Register with Crossref to assign DOIs directly through the journal (rather than only through Zenodo). This is a key credibility signal for academic indexing.
Q2 2026
Google Scholar and OpenAlex indexing Planned
Verify that published papers are discoverable in Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar, and OpenAlex. Add metadata tags to improve indexing coverage.
2026
Publish five or more papers Planned
Build a critical mass of published work. Several indexing services and directories (including DOAJ) require a minimum number of articles before applications can be considered.
Late 2026
Apply to DOAJ Planned
Apply to the Directory of Open Access Journals once the journal has an ISSN and a sufficient publication track record. DOAJ listing is the gold standard for open-access journal credibility.
2027+
Apply for Scopus and Web of Science Planned
After establishing a sustained publication record over 1–2 years, apply for indexing in Scopus and Web of Science. These require demonstrated editorial quality, regular publication, and international authorship.
